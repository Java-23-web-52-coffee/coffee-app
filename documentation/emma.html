Persona 1: Emma Student

Role: College Student

Technology: iPhone, MacBook Air, Google Maps, Spotify, productivity apps

Emma is a full-time student who spends several hours each week studying outside of class. She enjoys studying in coffee shops because they help her stay focused and productive. She frequently switches between locations depending on her workload and schedule.

She typically uses her phone to search for coffee shops while on campus and often visits locations she has never been to before.

Behaviors and Attitudes

  • Studies in coffee shops 3-5 times per week
  • Relies heavily on WiFi
  • Prefers quiet environments with minimal distractions
  • Values comfort and available seating
  • Often studies for several hours at a time
  • Enjoys trying new coffee shops but dislikes wasting time on places that do not meet her needs.

Scenario

Emma has a large exam coming up and needs a quiet coffee shop with reliable WiFi and available outlets so she can spend the afternoon studying.

She opens the App to find a location that matches her needs.

Frustrations

  • Online reviews rarely mention WiFi quality
  • Noise levels vary greatly from shop to shop
  • Arriving at a crowded coffee shop wastes valuable study time
  • It is difficult to know whether a location has enough outlets or seating

Goals

  • Find the best coffee shop for studying
  • Avoid noisy or crowded locations
  • Discover new study-friendly locations
  • Save time choosing where to work

Interaction Flow

Goal: Find a quiet coffee shop with reliable WiFi, available outlets, and study friendly for a 4-hour study session.

  • Emma opens the app on her phone between classes.
  • The app displays the Home Dashboard with search and recommendation options
  • Emma selects "Find a Coffee Shop."
  • App displays preference categories.
  • She selects: quiet atmosphere, strong WiFi, study friendly, available outlets
  • clicks "find matches"
  • App analyzes ratings and gives personalized recommendations
  • App displays ranked matches that include: distance, WiFi rating, noise rating
  • She selects the highest ranked match
  • App displays her chosen coffee shop's profile page
  • She saves this location to her favorites
  • After her study session, she reopens the app
  • App prompts her to rate her experience
  • The rating categories include: noise level, wifi quality, study friendliness, and seating comfort
  • She rates all categories and submits a review: "Quiet in the afternoon and plenty of seating"
  • App adds Emma's rating to the data base
