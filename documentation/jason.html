Persona : Jason Developer

Role: Remote Software Developer

Technology: Laptop, smartphone, Zoom, Slack, GitHub

Jason works remotely and occasionally leaves his home office to work from coffee shops. He often takes video calls, collaborates with teammates online, and spends most of the day connected to the internet.

Jason values comfortable seating, reliable WiFi, and a welcoming atmosphere. He frequently searches for locations where he can work productively while also enjoying a change of environment.

Behaviors and Attitudes

  • Works remotely 2-3 days per week from coffee shops
  • Uses coffee shops for focused work and virtual meetings
  • Prefers locations with strong internet and comfortable seating
  • Enjoys discovering local businesses
  • Frequently stays for several hours

Scenario

Jason has several virtual meetings scheduled and wants to find a coffee shop with strong WiFi, comfortable seating, and a professional atmosphere. He uses BrewMatch to compare nearby locations and choose the best option.

Frustrations

  • Generic review sites focus on food and drinks rather than productivity
  • Poor WiFi can disrupt meetings
  • Some coffee shops become crowded during peak hours

Goals

  • Find reliable remote-work-friendly coffee shops
  • Avoid locations with weak internet
  • Discover comfortable places to work for extended periods
  • Compare nearby locations quickly

Additional needs:

  • Customer reviews from other remote workers
  • Noise level ratings
  • Overall work-friendliness score
