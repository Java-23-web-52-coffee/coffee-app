Capstone Project: Sprint 1

This App will help students, remote workers, and coffee enthusiasts quickly discover coffee shops that match their specific needs and preferences. By combining community-driven insights, personalized recommendations, and data on factors such as WiFi quality, atmosphere, seating, and productivity, the platform simplifies the search process and helps users find their ideal coffee shop experience without sorting through hundreds of reviews.
